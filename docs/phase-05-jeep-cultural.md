# Phase 5 — Jeep / cultural sightseeing section (new)

Arlindo runs sightseeing tours in **Land Rovers** through a partner company. Positioned as the calm, cultural alternative to quads — critical for corporate customers, larger groups (20–50 people), and non-active audiences.

## Files

- `app/page.tsx` — new `<section id="sightseeing">` between the quad Signature Tour section and Reviews (or between Prices and Reviews, whichever reads better in build).

## Positioning

Key differentiators to make clear on the page:

- **You get driven**, not driving. No physical activity required.
- **Sintra (UNESCO patrimony)** — you actually visit the town, unlike the quad tours which only see it from a distance.
- **Cabo da Roca** — mainland Europe's westernmost point.
- **Wine cellar tasting** — Arlindo: "very difficult not to like it." Add-on options:
  - Simple: 1 tasting.
  - Full: wine tasting + cheese + bread.
- **Group capacity: 20–50 people** — call this out for corporate + large groups.
- **Alcohol OK** — because the customer isn't driving. Explicitly contrast with quads.
